# Project Brief

Client slug: `aqualoop-product-discovery`
Project name: AquaLoop Hospitality
Date: 2026-07-03

## Project Mandate

Create a complete Agency Discovery OS package for AquaLoop Hospitality: a serious industrial product discovery report and web deck preview for a potential client.

The output should help the client decide whether the product opportunity is real, which segment should come first, what assumptions require validation, what product architectures are plausible, which competitors and substitutes matter, and what is ready for internal discussion versus blocked for external use.

## Raw Idea

AquaLoop is an early product idea for capturing greywater from hotel or building sources such as showers, sinks, or other non-blackwater streams and redirecting it toward approved non-potable uses such as toilet flushing, cleaning, irrigation, or operational water reuse.

## Strategic Questions

1. Is hospitality actually the right first segment?
2. Who is the buyer and who influences the decision?
3. What use cases are strongest?
4. What product architecture options exist?
5. What technical unknowns could break the idea?
6. What regulatory, installation, maintenance, and manufacturing risks exist?
7. What competitors, adjacent analogues, substitutes, and status quo options matter?
8. What claims can be made safely?
9. What proof is missing?
10. What should the first MVP or pilot validate?
11. How should the product be positioned?
12. How should this be presented to partners, investors, or internal stakeholders?

## Candidate Segments

- Full-service hotels.
- Boutique hotels.
- Resorts.
- Multifamily residential buildings.
- Multifamily developers.
- Student housing.
- Gyms, spas, and wellness properties.
- Commercial buildings.
- Municipal or public facilities.

## Candidate Architecture Paths

- Modular unit per room.
- Centralized building system.
- Floor-by-floor semi-centralized system.
- Retrofit kit.
- New-build specification product.
- Service plus hardware model.
- Managed water-reuse service.
- Hybrid architecture selected by property type.
