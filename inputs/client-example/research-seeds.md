# Research Seeds

Client slug: `client-example`

## Priority Research Questions

1. How do enterprise field operations buyers describe coordination pain?
2. What language do competitors use to frame field service, workforce operations, and dispatch visibility?
3. Where does the market overemphasize software features instead of operational outcomes?
4. What proof points would make an enterprise operations executive believe a platform can reduce coordination risk?
5. How should implementation services be framed so they increase confidence rather than perceived complexity?

## Suggested Source Types

- Competitor websites and product pages
- Analyst summaries about field service management and connected operations
- Buyer review excerpts where allowed by license and source policy
- Client sales notes and win/loss data
- Customer support or success notes

## Known Competitor Categories

- Field service management suites
- Mobile workforce management tools
- Dispatch and scheduling platforms
- CRM or ERP workflow extensions
- Internal spreadsheet/process workarounds

## Internal Evidence To Request

- Win/loss summary by vertical
- Customer case studies
- Sales objection log
- Product usage metrics tied to operational outcomes
- Implementation timeline examples
