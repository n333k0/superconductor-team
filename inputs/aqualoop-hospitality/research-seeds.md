## Primary Research Questions

1. Are hotels a credible first segment for onsite greywater reuse compared with residential buildings or broader commercial buildings?
2. Which hotel buyer roles would own the problem, budget, risk, and approval path?
3. What competitors and substitutes already shape buyer expectations?
4. Which product architecture options are plausible, and what evidence is missing?
5. What regulatory, plumbing, water-quality, maintenance, and guest-experience risks could block adoption?
6. Which business models are possible without overclaiming savings or feasibility?
7. What claims can be safely made in a discovery deck today?
