# Strategy Deck Outline

Client slug: `client-example`
Date: 2026-07-03
Deck architect: Deck Architect
Intended audience: Northstar executive, marketing, sales, and customer success leaders
Decision the deck must unlock: Approve the execution confidence positioning and three-layer offer architecture for enterprise sales.

## Story Spine

1. Situation: Field operations teams are coordinating more work across more systems and regions.
2. Tension: The cost of missed handoffs shows up as customer escalations and executive uncertainty.
3. Evidence: Northstar's strongest sales moments already happen when buyers feel coordination pain.
4. Strategic choice: Lead with execution confidence, not generic workflow tooling.
5. Offer system: Package assessment, launch, and optimization as a clear buying path.
6. Activation path: Validate proof, prioritize verticals, and build the sales deck.
