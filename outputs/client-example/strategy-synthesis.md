# Strategy Synthesis

The strongest path is to make the offer easier to evaluate. The client should move from broad capability language toward a tighter story about the buyer's operating problem, the implementation path, and the measurable state after adoption.

## Recommended Emphasis

- Clarify the primary buyer and the moment of urgency.
- Replace generic claims with proof-backed operational outcomes.
- Separate internal discovery hypotheses from client-safe presentation claims.

## Review Notes

The strategy is suitable for agency review. External presentation should wait until revenue-impact language and customer reference permissions are confirmed.
